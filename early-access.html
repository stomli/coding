🎮 What Is Orb•Fall: ChromaCrush?

Orb•Fall is a fast-paced color-matching puzzle game inspired by classic block-droppers. Colorful orb pieces fall from the top of the grid — line up three or more matching colors to clear them, chain combos, and trigger explosions to rack up points before time runs out.

The game runs entirely in your browser with no app store required. You can install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your phone or desktop for a near-native experience, and once installed it works fully offline.

Currently optimized for Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Edge, Brave). Other browsers may have minor visual differences.

🕹️ Game Modes

Six distinct modes — each a fresh challenge. All modes track independent progress and high scores.

⚡ Classic

Timed 90-second rounds. Clear orbs fast, survive as long as you can. The best starting point for new players.

🧘 Zen

No timer, no pressure. Play at your own pace, save and resume anytime. Perfect for strategy and relaxation.

⚔️ Gauntlet

Start with a pre-filled grid and face a rising wave of random orbs every 5 seconds. Fast clears are essential.

🌊 Rising Tide

Immovable blocking orbs rise from the bottom every 5 seconds. Only Exploding orbs can remove them — plan wisely.

🎯 Mission

Complete a chain of sequential micro-goals — clear orbs, trigger cascades, use specials, build streaks. One goal revealed at a time.

🧩 Puzzle

No auto-drop, no timer. A deterministic piece sequence — same puzzle every time. Earn bronze, silver, or gold stars.

⚠️ Known Issues & Limitations

We want to be transparent about what's not quite ready yet:

  • Known
    Payments not connected Premium features and in-app purchases are visible in the UI but are not yet wired to a payment processor. Nothing will be charged.
  • Known
    Ads framework in place — no ads showing The ad infrastructure is integrated and the placements are live, but ad inventory is not yet active. You may see placeholder ad slots.
  • Roadmap
    Not yet available as a distributed app The game is currently web-only. App Store and Google Play distribution is planned for a future release.

🔒 Privacy — Quick Summary

No account required. Your game progress, settings, and player profiles are stored locally on your device using localStorage and are never sent to our servers.

Analytics. We collect anonymous gameplay analytics (events, session length, level completions) to understand how the game is played. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Ads. When live, ads may use third-party SDKs that set their own cookies or collect data per their own privacy policies.

No data is sold. We do not sell personal data to third parties.
