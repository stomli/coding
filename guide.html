🎮 Game Modes

Choose from four exciting game modes, each with unique challenges:

⚡ Classic

The original timed challenge! Clear orbs before time runs out. Master the art of quick combos and strategic placement to survive as long as possible.

🧘 Zen

No timer, no pressure! Play until the grid fills up. Perfect for practicing combos, testing strategies, or just relaxing with cascading orbs.

⚔️ Gauntlet

Start with 5 rows of pre-filled orbs at the bottom! As if that wasn't enough, a new row of random orbs rises from the bottom every 5 seconds, pushing everything up. Clear fast or face defeat!

🌊 Rising Tide

Rows of blocking orbs rise from the bottom every 5 seconds! These immovable obstacles push your orbs upward, adding constant pressure. Tip: Use Exploding orbs to destroy blocking orbs - it's the only way to clear them! Plan your moves carefully!

🎯 The Goal

Match as many orbs as possible by setting up massive cascades to achieve the highest score! Drop and rotate falling pieces strategically to create chain reactions where cleared orbs make new matches, multiplying your points with each cascade level. The bigger the cascade, the bigger your score!

🔗 How Matching Works

When 3 or more orbs of the same color touch horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, they disappear in a satisfying pop! Chain reactions create cascades for bonus points.

→

3 red orbs matched vertically = MATCH! 💥

⭐ Special Power Orbs

Special orbs appear with unique powers to help you clear the board and create massive combos!

Exploding Orb

When an exploding orb is part of any match, it creates a massive explosion that clears EVERYTHING - even blocking orbs!

Horizontal Painter

When this orb is in any match, it paints the entire row its color! Painted orbs instantly check for new matches.

Vertical Painter

When this orb is in any match, it paints the entire column its color! Perfect for setting up cascades.

Diagonal Painter

When this orb is in any match, it paints the NE-SW diagonal line (↗↙) its color! Creates unique cascade patterns.

Diagonal Painter

When this orb is in any match, it paints the NW-SE diagonal line (↖↘) its color! Creates unique cascade patterns.

Blocking Orb

Falls from the center - can't be matched! Only explosions can destroy them. Plan around them!

🔥 Pro Tips

  • Plan ahead! Use the ghost piece preview to set up massive cascades
  • Go vertical: Tall stacks create better cascade opportunities
  • Painter power: Use painters to instantly create matches and trigger cascades
  • Combo master: Each cascade level multiplies your score - aim for 3+ levels!
  • Center management: Keep the center clear for blocking orbs that fall from above
  • Difficulty = Multiplier: Higher difficulty = higher final scores!
